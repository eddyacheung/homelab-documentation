# Tailscale Remote Access

## Goal

Provide secure remote access to my homelab without exposing ports directly to the internet.

## Services Accessed

- UGREEN NAS
- Portainer
- Open WebUI
- Seerr

## Benefits

- Encrypted access
- No port forwarding required
- Easy remote management

## Lessons Learned

- Tailscale hostnames
- MagicDNS
- Remote Linux administration
- Secure access to self-hosted services
